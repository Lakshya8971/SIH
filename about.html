Our Mission and Vision

The Student Innovation Portal empowers students with resources to innovate in smart automation and AI. We focus on developing technologies that enhance safety and efficiency in smart vehicles.

Key Focus Areas

  • Smart Automation: Developing intelligent automation systems.
  • AI Integration: Leveraging AI for better insights and decision-making.
  • Vehicle Safety: Creating technologies to prevent accidents.
  • Sustainability: Promoting eco-friendly solutions.
